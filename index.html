CubeRover

Creative Direction Intern

CubeRover is a startup building a new generation of lunar rovers. I was one of the first five people brought on to the team after Astrobotic and Carnegie Mellon decided to spin out the project as its own company.

While working at CubeRover I developed the company branding from the ground up. I also designed and developed several iterations of the website, created video and animated content, and created rendered artwork of several rovers.

Finally I took point on the design of our user interface for the CubeRover back on earth.

Off-Beat

Service Design

Off-Beat is the result of a semester long project for a class on service design. My four teammates and I were tasked with designing a service centered around music.

Throughout the semester we did several rounds of interviews, speed dated concepts and explored business models to create a robust service people actually wanted to use.

Our process involved several pivots in concept but at the end we reached a polished design for each part of the service from the event hosts to our website.
